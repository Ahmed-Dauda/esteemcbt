Defense Day Tips
🎯
Know Your Problem Statement
Be able to explain why this research was necessary in 2–3 clear sentences.
📊
Own Your Methodology
Justify every research design choice — why this approach, why this sample size.
🤔
Acknowledge Limitations
Panels respect honesty. Know your study's weaknesses before they ask.
📚
Cite Confidently
Know your key references — author, year, and what they contribute to your work.
💬
"I Don't Know" Is Okay
Say: "That's beyond this study's scope, but a future researcher could explore..."
⏱
Practice Out Loud
Read each answer aloud 3× before the day. Hearing yourself builds confidence.
