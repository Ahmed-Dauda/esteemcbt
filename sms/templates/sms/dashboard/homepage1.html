Our Programs

Holistic Education at
Every Stage of Growth

From early childhood through secondary school — structured, values-driven education that equips every student for life.

Ages 1–2
Pre-Nursery

Esteem Pre-Nursery welcomes every baby into a well-established co-educational environment with specialist teachers, providing standard curricula that equip children from elementary level for nationwide success.

Ages 3–12
Nursery & Primary

From their first day, students benefit from excellent academics supported by highly qualified staff, plus a wide range of creative, sporting and artistic opportunities essential for all-round development.

Ages 12–18
Boarding Secondary

Esteem Boy's College integrates academic excellence, Islamic values, and discipline — earning its place among the nation's top international schools, renowned for academics, sports, and the arts.

Special
Esteem Q

A unique program where students memorize the Holy Qur'an within a year. Dedicated instructors and specialized curricula foster spiritual growth and a deep connection to the Holy Scriptures.
