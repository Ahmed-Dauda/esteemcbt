Pre-Nursery

Esteem Pre-Nursery School welcomes every baby to a well-established independent co-educational school with specialist teachers from Nigeria. We provide standard curriculums as well as a reputation for equipping children from an elementary level to a secondary level for success in order to compete with other students nationwide.

Nursery & Primary

From the very first moment a child enters Esteem School, he/she benefits from an excellent academic education supported by highly qualified and experienced specialist staff and a large range of creative, sporting and artistic opportunities which are essential for children’s all-round education and development.


Boarding Secondary

Esteem Boy’s College integrates academic excellence, Islamic values, and discipline, earning its place among the nation's top international schools, renowned for its comprehensive educational approach encompassing academics, sports, and the arts.



Esteem Q

Esteem Q offers a unique program where students memorize the Holy Qur'an within a year. Our dedicated instructors and specialized curriculum ensure that students achieve this remarkable feat while fostering spiritual growth and a deep connection to the Holy Scriptures.
